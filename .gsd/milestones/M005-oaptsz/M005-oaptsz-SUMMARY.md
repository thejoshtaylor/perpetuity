# M005-oaptsz: PWA, Notifications & Voice — UX layer that makes Perpetuity feel like a real phone product

**Converted Perpetuity into an installable PWA with a route-classified service worker, an end-to-end notification center (in-app + Web Push with VAPID + per-event preferences), and universal Grok STT voice input across every eligible text field — every existing flow audited at 360px on a four-project Playwright matrix.**
